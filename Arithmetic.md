## Commutative 
**Addition + Multiplication**
$$
5 + 6 = 6 + 5  \\
5 * 6 = 6 * 5
$$
  
## Associative 
**Addition + Multiplication**
$$
3 + (4 + 5) = (3 + 4) + 5
$$

## Distributive
**Mult, Left to Right**
$$ 
3 * (4 + 5) = (3 * 4) + (3 * 5) \\
3 * (4 - 5) = (3 * 4) - (3 * 5)
$$
**Divide, Right to Left**
$$
(4 + 5) / 3 = (4 / 3) + (5 / 3) \\
NOTE:\\
 3 / (4 +5) != (3 / 4) + (3 / 5)
$$
